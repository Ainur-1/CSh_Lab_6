To advertise means to make information public. Modern advertising has a task to show a product in a positive and pleasant way.
The advertisement has existed since antiquity time. At first there were murals that informed about gladiator fights.
It means that a product is shown in the movies. After that it gets well-known and well-sold. For example, it was made with the luxury cars which were shown in James Bond films.
